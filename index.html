Welcome to the Philadelphia Eagles fanpage! In this fanpage we have the current roster, the staff, info on the recent superbowl win, season predictions, and much more! You can navigate the website by the navigator above, in white.

The Eagles were founded in 1933 by Bert Bell, who would own the team from 1933-1940.
They are based in Philadelphia, PA.
The Eagles have 3 championships before the super-bowl era, in 1948, 1949, and 1960.
They only have 1 super-bowl win, from the 2017-2018 season.
