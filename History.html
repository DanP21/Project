The Eagles were founded in 1933 by Bert Bell. The Eagles have 3 national championships, 3 NFC championships, and 1 Superbowl win. The franchise has always been located in Philadelphia. The Eagles have bene a very successful franchise, they are worth over a billion dollars. They are one of the best teams in the NFL, and the best team in the NFC East as of 2018.
